Product

Our Product

For Accountants:

Spend your time using your expertise, not doing unnecessary manual work

  • Have all the relevant guidance summarized and linked to you in seconds: No more trawling through 1000s of pages of FASB or Big 4 documents.
  • View information only from trusted sources and latest regulations: No more worrying about looking stupid or having to redo your work because the guidance you're referring to is incorrect or out-of-date. Looking at you, ChatGPT.
  • Deliver a higher quality of work while achieving work-life-balance: No more late nights and weekends spent figuring out how to interpret your auditor's recommendations. Now you get it right before they need to correct you.

We can't use AI to automate fulfill audit requirements but we can use it to automate the most time-intensive steps so that you can use your energy on the parts that matter most.



For Companies:

Increase efficiency and reduce costs while keeping your workforce happy

  • Enable your accounting team to do more for a fraction of the cost of hiring a new employee. We cut away the most low-value and time-intensive work so your accountants can work on what matters.
  • Benefit from the advances in AI without the risk. We're designed to help your accounting team find and understand trusted information as quickly as possible - how your team decides to use that information is still up to them.
  • Reduce the cost of inadequately staffed accounting teams. When accounting teams are burnt out, there is a high risk of attrition and a high risk of mistakes. Both of which are extremely costly: (a) Hiring and onboarding new accountants during the current CPA shortage is difficult and expensive (b) Having insufficiently kept books can lead to extended audits, reissuing of financial statements, and more

We are the way to benefit from AI without sharing your sensitive information or exposing yourself to unnecessary risk.
